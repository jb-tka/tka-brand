## 1. Who we are
TKA is a private, nonprofit, Pre-K–12, college-preparatory **Christian** school in West Palm Beach, FL,
serving early childhood through 12th grade. Directed by a Board of Governors; led by President
**Randal L. ("Randy") Martin** and the Administrative Team.
- **Tagline:** "Where Christ is King."
- **Motto:** *Fiat Lux* — "let there be light." Jesus is the light of the world; all subject matter
  is meant to harmonize with Christ and the Bible.
- **Mission:** to assist the home and church in their endeavor to "train up a child in the way he
  should go" (Proverbs 22:6) — sharing salvation through Jesus Christ and graduating Christian
  leaders who impact their world through academic excellence and spiritual vitality.
- **Vision (brief):** a distinctively Christian, world-class education from highly qualified Christian
  educators, building character, scholarship, and work ethic, equipping students as local-to-global
  leaders. Full text: https://www.tka.net/about-tka

## 2. Voice & tone
- **Warm, personal, faith-forward, excellence-minded.** The President's own writing is the model:
  caring and testimonial, "reach the heart before teaching the mind," confident about TKA's quality
  without boasting.
- **Preserve the individual's voice.** When drafting an admin's email, match *their* style — the
  house style is the floor, not a flattening.

## 3. Faith
Faith is integral, not decorative. TKA is unapologetically Christian.
- **Doctrinal frame (summary):** authority and inerrancy of Scripture (all 66 books); the Triune God;
  the deity, virgin birth, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and return of Jesus Christ; the Holy
  Spirit indwelling believers; creation by intelligent design; the Fall and humanity's need for
  salvation; salvation by grace through faith alone in Christ; the Church as the body of Christ; the
  call to holy living and the Great Commission; marriage as a covenant of one man and one woman; and
  the sanctity of all human life from conception to natural death. The Board of Governors is the final
  authority on doctrine. Canonical text:
  https://www.tka.net/about-tka/from-the-president/statement-of-faith
- **Institutional values:** keeping focus on Christ; inspiring academic achievement; nurturing
  students; growing as teachers and leaders; serving families.
- **The dial (default):** natural and sincere in parent/community/mission content; light-touch in
  routine operational/internal email — present but not performative. Quote Scripture accurately or
  not at all.

## 4. Naming & terminology
- **The King's Academy** on first reference; **TKA** thereafter.
- Say **"AI projects / initiatives,"** never **"processes."**
- Products: **TKA Tix** (ticketing), **TK Bus** (bus communication/GPS app).
- **The King's Online Academy** (TKOA) — full name first, TKOA after. Public site: kingsonlineacademy.com
- **Mascot:** Lion(s). **School colors (digital palette):** Royal Blue `#003580`, Red `#D21034`, White `#FFFFFF` — use these for text/headers/shapes. (Full specs + logo-raster notes in `tka-brand/brand/colors.md`.)
- Sub-brands with their own marks: King's Service Organization, King's Corner, Early Childhood Program,
  Spiritual Life, The Institute for Christ & Culture, Smith Family Bistro, athletics/Lions.
- **Naming people:** internal/staff comms use first names; formal or parent/external comms use full
  name + title on first reference. See roster below.

## 5. Audiences
- **Parents / families** — warmest, most faith-forward, jargon-free.
- **Admins / staff (internal)** — direct and efficient; "projects," tempered re: AI.
- **Prospective families (marketing / TKOA)** — mission-forward, benefit-led.
- **Board / leadership** — concise, outcomes-first.
