# TKA 2026-2027 Events — Plain-Text Calendar

> **What this is:** a single, machine-readable list of every event on TKA's
> 2026-2027 calendars, so an admin's Claude can answer questions like
> *"What's going on this week at TKA?"* without opening a PDF.
>
> **How to read it:** dates are ISO (`YYYY-MM-DD`) for easy date math. The
> academic year runs **July 2026 → July 2027**. Three sections below:
> the **K-12 School Calendar** and the **Early Childhood Program (ECP) Calendar**
> are **date-only** (the source calendars list no times or locations); the
> **Employee Orientation Week** section includes **times and locations** because
> the source schedule provides them. For a given week, scan all three sections.
>
> **Sources** (in this same `schedules/` folder, the authoritative originals):
> `2026-2027 School Calendar.pdf` (rev. 6/18/2026),
> `2026-2027 Early Childhood Program Calendar.pdf` (rev. 4/16/2026),
> `2026-2027 Orientation Schedule.pdf` (upd. 5/12/2026).
> **All dates subject to change** — the live calendars at **TKA.net** /
> **TKAECP.net** are the final word. Regenerate this file when a PDF is updated.
>
> **2026-2027 School Verse —** *"May the God of hope fill you with all joy and
> peace as you trust in Him, so that you may overflow with hope by the power of
> the Holy Spirit."* (Romans 15:13)
